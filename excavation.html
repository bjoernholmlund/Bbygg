Excavation


Holmlund Contracting provides complete contracting services for excavation and planning for all types of projects. We operate throughout Sweden and have the capacity to handle large-scale jobs. To establish a solid foundation, every construction requires a firm and stable base. We specialize in rough excavation for larger projects, which includes the removal of substantial quantities of excavated materials. We have the capability to manage the transportation of large volumes of excavated material with our own dump sites. We perform excavation work across the entire country. A project is only as good as its foundational work—feel free to reach out for more information about our services.

Extent of excavation

The extent of excavation required depends on the necessary depth, influenced by soil conditions and the risk of frost. Cold climates, large constructions, and significant amounts of topsoil necessitate a deeper foundation. We efficiently manage the fill material and transport it away as needed. Additionally, we have the resources to deliver gravel for various types of foundations and reinforcement layers. We undertake all types of excavation and drainage work, specializing in larger projects.

We take environmental work seriously

At Holmlund Contracting, we take our environmental responsibilities seriously. We handle materials carefully, ensuring proper disposal and recycling. We are certified according to ISO 14001, which mandates continuous improvement in our environmental practices. Our fleet includes one of Sweden's most modern and resource-efficient machinery, which helps us reduce fuel consumption and minimize carbon emissions during our work. These advanced machines meet the toughest environmental classifications and requirements.
