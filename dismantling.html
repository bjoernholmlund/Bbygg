Dismantling


We carry out dismantling and reuse of various building elements and structures.


Dismantling of machines and equipment


We assist you with machine dismantling and the disassembly of various equipment and buildings, always keeping economy and efficiency in mind. We manage the entire process, including project planning, dismantling work, packaging, labeling, and transportation to the desired delivery location. In cases where complete facilities are not fully dismantled, but only specific parts are sent for machine dismantling, these components are disassembled and sold as spare parts. Other materials are demolished and scrapped. We ensure that the scrapped materials are sorted carefully to maximize economic returns and minimize environmental impact.

Dismantling with Excavators


No part is too small or too large to be salvaged. From both an economic and environmental standpoint,
there is significant value in using excavators for dismantling, as it facilitates the careful recovery of materials.
With our fleet of both large and small machines, we can efficiently salvage valuable materials while ensuring
everything is properly sorted. We have the experience and expertise needed to effectively salvage all components
of a building, reclaiming and reusing trusses, modules, bridge parts, and more. Contact us at Holmlund Contracting
for more information about dismantling machinery and facilities, as well as demolition and excavation services.
